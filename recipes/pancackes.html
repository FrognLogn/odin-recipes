Pancakes Recipe

Description

A liquid that slightly rises in a pan to create a soft sugary circular doughy food.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Pancake mix
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Add measured amount of mix and water into a bowl
  2. Mix together ingredients in bowl until good consistency
  3. Pour unto pan which has been oiled
  4. Once bottom is bit browned, flip over the pancake
  5. Plate, add butter and syrup if you wish
