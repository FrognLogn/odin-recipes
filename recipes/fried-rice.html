Fried Rice

Description

Wok-tossed rice that is fried then mixed with many vegetables, spices, and a sauce.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Meat of your choosing
  • Soy sauce
  • Green onions
  • Green peas

Steps

  1. Fry the rice and add sauce
  2. Cut up all the vegetables
  3. Add vegetables to the rice
  4. Enjoy good food
